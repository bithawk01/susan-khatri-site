Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information on this website.

Last updated: January 1, 2025
// 01

Introduction

Welcome to Susan Khatri Tools Hub, accessible at susan-khatri.com.np. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your personal data.

By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the site.

This website is operated by Susan Khatri, based in Nepal. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at the email listed on our Contact page.
// 02

Information We Collect

We collect two types of information: information you provide directly, and information collected automatically when you browse the site.

Information you provide:

  • Your name and email address if you submit our contact form
  • Any message or content you send through the contact form

Information collected automatically:

  • Your IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type, operating system, and device information
  • Pages you visit and how long you spend on each page
  • The website that referred you to ours
  • Clicks on download links and buttons
// 03

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. This website uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies — used by Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site
  • Advertising cookies — used by Google AdSense to show relevant advertisements

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect some functionality. Most browsers allow you to refuse new cookies, delete existing cookies, and be warned when a new cookie is set.

// 04

Google AdSense & Advertising

This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense is an advertising service provided by Google LLC.

Google uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other sites across the internet. This is known as interest-based advertising.

  • Google may use the DoubleClick cookie to serve ads on this site
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your visit to this site
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings
  • You can also opt out via the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page
We do not control the advertisements shown by Google AdSense. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this and other websites. For more information, see Google's advertising privacy policy.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit the site, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to coming to this site.

Google Analytics collects only the IP address assigned to you on the date you visit this site, rather than your name or other identifying information. We do not combine the information collected through Google Analytics with personally identifiable information.

  • Data collected is used solely to improve the website's content and user experience
  • Google Analytics data is retained for 26 months by default
  • You can prevent Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on
